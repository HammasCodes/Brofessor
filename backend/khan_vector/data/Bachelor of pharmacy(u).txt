Programme Information: Graduate Programme (Department of Pharmacy)

Programme Code: PHA-001

Duration: 4 Years

Department: Department of Pharmacy

Programme Fee Per Year:

1st Year: ₹1,40,000

2nd Year: ₹1,40,000

3rd Year: ₹1,40,000

4th Year: ₹1,40,000

Eligibility Criteria:

Regular Admission: 50% (45% for SC/ST) Marks in 10+2 level with PCM/PCB.

Lateral Entry to Second Year: 60% (55% for SC/ST) Marks in Diploma in Pharmacy.

Age criteria as per PCI norms.

Admission Procedure: On the basis of Merit/IUCET/State-Level and National-Level Entrance Exams.
