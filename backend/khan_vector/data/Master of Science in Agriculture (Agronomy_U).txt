Programme Information: Post Graduate Programme (Department of Agriculture)

Programme Code: MSC-008

Duration: 2 Years

Department: Department of Agriculture

About the Programme:
Agronomy is the branch of agricultural science that deals with principles and practices of soil, water, crop and nutrient management. It involves the sustainable management of complex physical, chemical, and climatological environments with the dual aim of improving agricultural production and productivity.

Programme Fee Per Year:

1st Year: ₹50,000

2nd Year: ₹50,000

Eligibility Criteria:
B.Sc. Agriculture / B.Sc. (Hons.) [Agriculture/Forestry].

Admission Procedure:
On the basis of Merit, CUET, or IUCET.
